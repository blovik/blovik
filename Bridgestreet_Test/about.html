About Bridge Street Bargains

Where? Visalia, CA

Visalia is situated in the middle of the San Jaquin valley in central California. To our east we daily gaze at the granduer of the Sierra mountains, containing the world famous redwood trees. To our east we have the coastal range and then the coast with its beautiful beaches. Here in Tulare county we supply the world with fruit, nuts, grapes and grains.

Who? The Sawyers

Our family moved into the area in the 1930's. My wife, Cathy, and I have raise two wonderful daughters who are the pride of our lives. They even volunteer time to help out with the store.

Some years ago I discovered that I have a knack for finding vintage furniture and other collectables. I travel all over the area attending and conducting estate sales and auctions. I recognize quality items and good prices. I think you will like my selection.

If you are looking for an item, email me or give me a call and I will keep my eyes open for the treasure you seek.

Darin Sawyer
