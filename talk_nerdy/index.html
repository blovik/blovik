Talk Nerdy

talk

  1. speak in order to give information or express ideas or feelings; converse or communicate by spoken words: the two men talked | we'd sit and talk about jazz | it was no use talking to Anthony | [ with obj. ] : you're talking rubbish.
  2. have the power of speech: he can talk as well as you or I can.
  3. discuss personal or intimate feelings: we need to talk, Maggie.

Talk Nerd-y

nerd

noun informal

  1. a foolish or contemptible person studious: one of those nerds who never asked a girl to dance.
  2. a single-minded expert in a particular technical field: a computer nerd.
